AI Onboarding Workshop｜AI 入门工作坊

AI Prompt Thinking Class

A practical onboarding class to help participants shift from a chat approach to a prompt approach, and understand how prompting with AI tools can accelerate productivity at work and in life.

From Chat → Prompt → Loop｜从聊天到提示，再到迭代

Think Better. Prompt Better. Work Faster.

This workshop introduces AI Prompt Thinking as a practical way to ask better questions, design better prompts, and turn AI tools into a thinking partner for daily work, business decisions, learning and personal productivity.

这堂 AI 入门课帮助参与者从“跟 AI 聊天”转向“用 Prompt 构建思维”，学习如何通过更清晰的提示设计，让 AI 工具加速工作、学习、决策与生活效率。

01 Chat Notice what you ask and why.｜看见自己如何提问
02 Prompt Design context, role, output and thinking structure.｜设计背景、角色、输出与思考结构
03 Loop Refine, evaluate and apply in real work.｜迭代、评估并应用到真实工作

Forum Intent｜论坛目的

  • Create meaningful conversations about AI beyond technology.
  • Understand how AI impacts different industries and professions.
  • Encourage responsible, practical and human-centered AI adoption.
  • Connect businesses, professionals and communities to learn together.

Expected Outcomes｜预期成果

  • Clearer understanding of AI impact across industries.
  • Authentic perspectives from diverse business and community sectors.
